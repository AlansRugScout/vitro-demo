Our Platform

Built for the way clinicians actually work

Every Vitro product starts with clinical insight — not IT theory. The result is software that gets adopted, not abandoned.

Core Product

Electronic Medical Record (EMR)

Vitro's EMR is designed around your existing paper forms — preserving the familiarity that drives adoption while adding powerful digital capabilities that paper can never offer.

  • Retain existing paper documents as digital forms — zero retraining
  • Real-time validation, calculations, and automated alerts
  • Patient chartbook — scroll through complete history page by page
  • Charting, drawing, photo capture, and boilerplate text
  • Mobile access — clinicians access records on the move
  • Rapid deployment: from sign-off to live in under 6 months
📋

Digital replica of your existing clinical forms

Data & Analytics

Clinical Data Management

Centralise every patient record across departments, wards, and facilities into a single, searchable, secure platform. Give your team the 360° patient view they need to make better decisions faster.

  • 360-degree patient view across all departments
  • Powerful search, sort, and filter across entire patient history
  • Real-time reporting and outcome analytics
  • Secure, auditable access with role-based permissions
  • You own your data — full open access export at any time

Real-time clinical dashboards and reporting

Integration

Systems Interoperability

Vitro is designed to work alongside your existing technology investments — not replace them. With HL7 FHIR support and open APIs, Vitro connects to lab systems, imaging, billing, and third-party platforms seamlessly.

  • HL7 FHIR compliant — connects to any modern health system
  • Pre-built connectors for major laboratory and imaging systems
  • Bi-directional data flow — no duplicate data entry
  • Scales from single-site clinics to 15-hospital groups
  • Cloud-hosted with enterprise-grade security and uptime

About Vitro Software

Founded by clinicians. Built for healthcare.

Since 2006, we've been on a mission to eliminate the inefficiency of paper in hospitals — improving outcomes for patients and professionals alike.

Our Story

A leading international healthcare technology company

Vitro Software was founded in 2006 with a simple but powerful idea: that the best digital health tools are designed by people who understand clinical environments, not just software engineers.

Our first product, Claimsure, transformed revenue cycle management in Irish hospitals, replacing slow paper-based processes with a fully electronic system now used in over 90% of Irish hospitals.

Today, we deliver electronic medical records, clinical data management, and systems integration to healthcare organisations across Ireland, Australia, New Zealand, and beyond.
